About Dinar Connection

Dinar Connection is a fun and educational game designed to enhance your financial intelligence by having more control on your spending behavior and being more wise in managing between your needs and desires!

Join us and discover the world of the dinar through engaging gameplay!

Key Features:

  • players will recognize more manageable ways in controlling their budgets after knowing the value of the Bahrani dinar
  • players will have the skills in how to allocate funds wisely, prioritize spending, and understanding the importance of budgeting
  • players will become more familiar with the currency, improving their ability to handle money in real-life situations
  • players will Track their progress on the leaders board
  • players will be rewarded after Completing daily challenges will be giving to them as a task of the day
