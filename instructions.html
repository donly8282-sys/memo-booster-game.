Memory Matching Game Instructions

The objective of the Memory Matching Game is to find all matching pairs of influencer cards.

How to Play

  1. Start the Game: Open the game in your web browser by launching index.html.
  2. Game Setup: The game consists of 24 cards, with 12 unique influencers appearing twice.
  3. Flipping Cards:
    • Click on a card to flip it and reveal the influencer's image.
    • Click on a second card to reveal another image.
  4. Making Matches:
    • If the two flipped cards show the same influencer, they will remain flipped.
    • If they do not match, they will flip back after a short delay.
  5. Winning the Game: Continue flipping cards until all pairs are found. Once all pairs are matched, a win message will be displayed.
  6. Play Again: Click the "Play Again" button to restart the game.

Tips

  • Try to remember the locations of the cards you've flipped to make matching easier.
  • Take your time; there's no rush!
